Simple Syrup

Description

Learn how to make simple syrup to use in homemade cocktails and other drink recipes. It's easy to make with just two ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine equal amounts of sugar and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Add the spinach and cream.
  4. Allow to cool and store in the fridge in a clean jar with a tight-fitting lid.
